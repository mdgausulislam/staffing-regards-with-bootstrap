How it Works?

Client & Candidate Referrals

Watch a Short Video

Refer

You may refer Job Requirements from either Direct Clients or Tier 1 Vendors. Additionally, consultants who wish to transfer billing to our company are welcome. You may also look into opportunities for BPO/Outsourcing in various fields such as IT, Healthcare, and many others.

Relax

Once you send a referral, we will take care of the entire process including sourcing, recruiting, and placements. This also encompasses HR and administrative functions, such as handling the immigration process for billing transfers. Regular status updates on all these matters will be provided to you through your partner portal.

Reward

When a client successfully engages in a project, your portal will reflect the income generated, in line with the agreement for that specific project. Details regarding payout schedules will also be made available on the partner portal. Please be aware that we facilitate payments worldwide using bank wire transfer.

Bonus Referrals/Link Sharing

You will receive a small referral fee when partners join StaffingRewards.com using your unique personal link or code.

After a partner is approved, you will also be paid a one-time incentive for any business that is referred by that partner.
